FACTION_INFO_UNION|The Apostatic Union was formed roughly forty years ago, after its various member realms defeated the Wikkans in the God War. Their shared belief is that, for humanity to prosper, gods and god-worshippers must be expelled from human society. In the Union, to call someone 'devout' is an insult indeed.||||
FACTION_INFO_WIKKAN|Even before the World Rend, Wikkan covens dominated the sky realms. The various Wikkan covens would negotiate pious contracts between men and gods, holding each to strict codes of conduct. The Apostatic Union, seeking the freedom to pursue their own study of cipher magic, defeated the Wikkans in the God War; and now their power is much diminished.||||
FACTION_INFO_OUTLAW|A catchall term for the various brigands and ruffians inhabiting the Oneiric Isles. Being on the fringes of Apostatic Union territory, those islands have been relatively lawless until recently; with numerous criminal gangs finding safe haven there. Though they are often in competition with one another, word travels fast among the Outlaw gangs and it is possible to garner a reputation as a mercenary of note.||||
FACTION_INFO_DARKSTAR|A mercenary band led by Vela Callose, the Dark Star Company is infamous across the Apostatic Union for their villainous deeds. It is said they will do anything for coin, and that they have amassed vast funds. They were last seen on the Oneiric Isles.||||
FACTION_INFO_ENDLESS1|Loyal to the Queen of Silver, the Endless Royalists believe in honouring their monarch's intent. This includes feeding upon Penitent Thaw's mindless meat in place of sentient flesh. Such a stance has helped repair relations with mortal factions in the skyrealms over the past century or so.||||
FACTION_INFO_ENDLESS2|The Endless Cadavers are a shadowy bunch, loosely organised under Lord Rhydder's command. They believe in returning to the dark days of the Cadaver Crusades, in which the endless would invade neighbouring islands and feast upon mortal flesh. This would, of course, be in stark defiance to the wishes of the Queen of Silver.||||
